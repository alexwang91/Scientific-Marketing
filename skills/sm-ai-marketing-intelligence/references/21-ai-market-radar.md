# 21 AI Market Radar

## Core Position

Market shifts show up in language before they show up in reports. AI helps collect and cluster signals, but the team must decide which signals matter.

## Use When

Use this for category trend detection, competitor language, user needs, emerging objections, and AI answer monitoring.

## Method

1. Define the category and question.
2. List signal sources.
3. Collect raw language and examples.
4. Cluster signals by job, anxiety, use case, claim, or competitor.
5. Turn signals into hypotheses.

## Sources

Reviews, forums, sales notes, support tickets, competitor sites, retailer pages, YouTube and TikTok comments, app stores, search suggestions, AI answer engines, and analyst reports when available.

## Outputs

- Signal board.
- Language shifts.
- Competitor claim map.
- Opportunity hypotheses.

## Validation

A signal matters when it repeats across sources, ties to a buying situation, or explains a business metric.

## Failure Modes

- Mistaking novelty for demand.
- Treating social buzz as willingness to pay.
- Overweighting one platform's audience.

## Language Notes

Say "signal" until the team verifies demand.
