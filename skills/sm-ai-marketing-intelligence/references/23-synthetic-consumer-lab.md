# 23 Synthetic Consumer Lab

## Core Position

Synthetic consumers generate hypotheses. They do not represent customers.

## Use When

Use this to prepare interviews, stress-test concepts, create objections, explore message variants, or simulate buying situations before real research.

## Do Not Use When

Do not use synthetic consumers to estimate market size, conversion, willingness to pay, or final positioning.

## Method

1. Define the segment and buying situation.
2. State the assumptions used to create the synthetic profile.
3. Run concept, message, objection, and purchase path simulations.
4. Convert outputs into hypotheses.
5. Decide the real validation method.

## Validation

Validate with customer interviews, behavioral data, experiments, expert review, or historical data.

## Outputs

- Synthetic interview notes.
- Objection list.
- Concept risk list.
- Hypothesis backlog.

## Failure Modes

- Calling AI personas research.
- Inventing precise numbers.
- Letting the model mirror the team's desired answer.

## Language Notes

Use "synthetic response" and "hypothesis." Do not use "customer evidence" for model output.
