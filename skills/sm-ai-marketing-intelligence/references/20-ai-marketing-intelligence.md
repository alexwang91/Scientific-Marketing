# 20 AI Marketing Intelligence

## Core Position

AI helps marketing in five roles: radar, miner, simulator, creator, and operator. It does not replace market judgment or causal measurement.

## Contains

Market radar, insight miner, synthetic consumer lab, creative lab, agentic marketing ops, personalization inputs, and AI-assisted monitoring.

## Use When

Use this when the user asks how AI can improve marketing work, not just write copy.

## Method

1. Decide which AI role fits the task.
2. Identify the input data.
3. Define what AI may generate.
4. Define what humans must judge.
5. Define the validation path.

## Outputs

- AI opportunity map.
- Workflow design.
- Input and output specification.
- Validation plan.

## Validation

AI has helped only when it changes a decision, reduces manual work, finds a signal humans missed, or creates variants that a test can compare.

## Failure Modes

- Calling generic content generation a marketing strategy.
- Using synthetic consumers as proof.
- Automating budget, audience, or claims without approval.

## Language Notes

Avoid "AI-powered" unless you name the step AI performs.
