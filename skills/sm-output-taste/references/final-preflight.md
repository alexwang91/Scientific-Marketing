# Final Preflight

## Core Position

Ship answers that a marketing team can use.

## Check

Before final output, verify:

1. The answer identifies the business decision.
2. Important claims have evidence labels.
3. AI output is marked as hypothesis unless validated.
4. Causal claims have control, holdout, experiment, or credible counterfactual.
5. Recommendations name owner, action, metric, and next step when possible.
6. No generic consulting language remains.
7. Professional terms carry method.
8. Governance risks are named for personalization, nudges, claims, or agents.
9. The user asked for every module or file, and each item is present.
10. No "and so on" replaces actual content.

## Rewrite Triggers

Rewrite any sentence that could fit any brand. Rewrite any paragraph that sounds polished but does not change a decision.
