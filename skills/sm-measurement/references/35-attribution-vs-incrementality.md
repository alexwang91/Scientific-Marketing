# 35 Attribution vs Incrementality

## Core Position

Attribution explains which touchpoint gets credit in a reporting system. Incrementality estimates what would not have happened without the action.

## Use When

Use this when stakeholders argue over platform ROAS, last-click, view-through conversions, affiliate credit, or retargeting.

## Method

1. Identify the attribution rule.
2. Identify likely selection bias.
3. Ask what the counterfactual is.
4. Propose holdout, geo test, or suppression test.
5. Compare reported conversions to incremental conversions.

## Outputs

- Attribution risk note.
- Incrementality test proposal.
- Budget implication.

## Validation

Channels with high attribution and low incrementality should lose budget or change role.

## Failure Modes

- Funding retargeting because last-click ROAS looks high.
- Double-counting conversions across platforms.
- Ignoring organic demand.

## Language Notes

Say "credit" for attribution and "value" for incrementality.
