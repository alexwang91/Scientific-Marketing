# 45 Claim Risk

## Core Position

A claim needs proof. AI-generated claims need stricter review because they can sound precise without being true.

## Use When

Use this for product claims, AI feature claims, health, safety, privacy, productivity, performance, comparison, and sustainability.

## Method

1. Extract every claim.
2. Classify claim type.
3. Attach proof.
4. Check qualifiers and limits.
5. Decide whether legal, product, or compliance review is required.

## Outputs

- Claim table.
- Proof requirement.
- Revised copy.
- Approval needs.

## Validation

The claim should survive a competitor challenge and customer complaint.

## Failure Modes

- Claiming "best" without basis.
- Making productivity claims without measurement.
- Using AI to invent proof.
- Hiding limits in footnotes.

## Language Notes

Use "supported by" only when proof exists.
